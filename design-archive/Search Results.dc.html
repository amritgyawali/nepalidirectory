Top Kathmandu Restaurant FAQs

Where can I find authentic Newari food in Kathmandu? −

Some of the most authentic Newari restaurants in Kathmandu are Newa Lahana in Bhaktapur, Bhojan Griha in Dilli Bazar, Lhasa Newari Restaurant in Patan, and Honacha in Patan Durbar Square. For samay baji, chatamari and choila, locals recommend Newa Lahana's heritage tasting set.
