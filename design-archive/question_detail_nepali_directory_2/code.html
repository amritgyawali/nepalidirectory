Does Newa Lahana in Bhaktapur accept reservations on weekends?

S
Sujan K. asked this

4 Answers

sort Sort by: Most Helpful
Anita Maharjan verified Local Expert
Answered 2 days ago
42

Yes, they do accept reservations on weekends, but I highly recommend calling them at least 1-2 days in advance, especially if you plan to visit on a Saturday afternoon. Saturday lunch is their peak time, and the rooftop seating (which has the best views of Bhaktapur) gets completely booked out by locals and tourists alike.

If you don't have a reservation, you might end up waiting 30-45 minutes for a table. You can reach them directly at their main line. Enjoy the Samay Baji, it's totally worth the visit!
