About Newa Lahana

Newa Lahana is a heritage Newari restaurant set inside an 18th-century Malla-era courtyard in Bhaktapur Durbar Square. Founded in 2008 by chef Sumesh Maharjan, the restaurant serves traditional Newari fine dining with an emphasis on samay baji, choila and seasonal Newari festivals (Mha Puja, Yomari Punhi, Bhintuna). The 12-course tasting menu (Rs 2,400/person) features dishes rarely seen outside Newari homes — sapu mhicha, kachila and the seasonal pukala. Aila and chyaang are house-fermented and served in traditional anti-clay pots.
