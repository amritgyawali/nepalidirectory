Yes, they do accept reservations on weekends, but you need to call them well in advance (usually by Thursday) because Saturday afternoons are incredibly busy with locals and tourists.

You can reach them directly at 01-66XXXXX or 9841XXXXXX. If you are a large group (more than 8 people), they might ask for a small advance payment via eSewa to confirm the traditional floor-seating areas.

Pro tip: Ask for the rooftop seating if you're booking for the late afternoon—the sunset view over the Bhaktapur rooftops is amazing while enjoying your Chhoila and Aila!
