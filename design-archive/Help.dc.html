How can we help?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my business listing?

Search for your business in the directory. If it exists, click the "Claim this listing" button on the business detail page. If it doesn't exist, you can add it through your Dashboard.

Is it free to list my business?

Yes! A basic business listing is 100% free forever. You can also upgrade to premium features to get more visibility.

How do I edit my business information?

Log in to your account, go to the Dashboard, and select "My Listings". From there you can update your hours, phone number, and address.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Go to the Login page and click on "Forgot Password" to receive a password reset link in your email.

Still need help?

Our support team is available Monday-Friday, 9am - 5pm NPT.
